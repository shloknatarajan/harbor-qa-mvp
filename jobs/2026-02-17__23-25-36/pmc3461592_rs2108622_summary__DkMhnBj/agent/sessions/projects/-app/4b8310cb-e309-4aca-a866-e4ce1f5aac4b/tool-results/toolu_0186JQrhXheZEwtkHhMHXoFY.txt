248:CYP4F2 is clearly involved in warfarin metabolism. In addition to rs2108622 Val/Met, the rs3093105 Trp/Gly is a non-synonymous substitution and might be functional [[26]](#pone.0105250-Liu1) but we did not observe its association with warfarin dose. In related genes such as *GGCX*, *CALU*, *CYP2C19*, *EPHX1*, *F2*, *F5*, *F11*, and *PROCR*, we could not confirm the previous associations of these SNPs with warfarin dose in our Chinese population.
