172-The trim-and-fill analysis suggested that five studies were missing. The adjusted normalized difference in means per the random-effects summary was 7.1% (95% CI: 4.2–10.0), obtained after symmetrically filling the funnel plot. This indicated that the strength of the association with mean coumarin drug maintenance dose may be overestimated (by 12%) because of unpublished studies.
